# Maintainer Delegation and Rotation

**Status:** Normative authority specification

## 1. Maintainer meaning

A maintainer coordinates a bounded domain. A maintainer does not own code, contributors, a branch, a trademark, or a permanent seat.

## 2. Delegation object

Maintainer authority MUST be created by a signed delegation specifying:

- recipient key lineage;
- working group and domain;
- allowed actions;
- risk ceiling;
- term start and expiry;
- review obligations;
- conflict requirements;
- subdelegation rule;
- suspension and revocation procedure;
- delegating authority and policy version.

## 3. Eligibility evidence

Selection SHOULD consider:

- demonstrated domain competence;
- accurate reviews;
- integration reliability;
- constitutional understanding;
- security behavior;
- availability;
- ability to explain decisions;
- independence from proposal authors where required.

Commit count, wealth, employer, fame, legal identity, and AI output volume MUST NOT independently determine authority.

## 4. Terms

All delegations MUST expire. Renewal requires fresh evidence and review. Terms may overlap to preserve continuity but SHOULD avoid synchronized total turnover.

## 5. Rotation

Rotation may be ordinary, voluntary, inactivity-based, or corrective. Outgoing maintainers MUST transfer open obligations, private security material under controlled procedure, and integration context.

## 6. Suspension

Immediate temporary suspension is permitted for key compromise, credible malicious activity, severe undisclosed conflict, or inability to meet quorum. Suspension MUST be narrow, time-bounded, reviewable, and signed.

## 7. Removal

Removal requires evidence and an appeal path, except automatic expiry or cryptographic key revocation. Removal of authority does not erase accepted contributions or strip unrelated identity rights.

## 8. Emergency replacement

Emergency replacement MUST use a predeclared succession or higher-level governance process. A single founder/admin fallback may exist only during an explicitly documented bootstrap phase and must have an exit plan.

## 9. AI

AI may perform triage, review, testing, and coordination. It cannot hold a maintainer delegation that counts toward human/governance quorum under the current constitutional model. A human or authorized persistent pseudonym remains responsible for delegated decisions.
